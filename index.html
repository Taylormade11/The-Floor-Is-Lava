Welcome to The Floor is Lava!

    Can you make it to the top?!

Instructions

Your goal is to reach the top of the screen and pass through the goal.

There’s 40 seconds on the clock and pointsare being deducted for every second you wait so we suggest you move fast!

Falling in the lava stops the clock, resultingin zero points.

Don’t forget to enter your name at the end to see how you rank against our top players.

Remember

DON’T FALLIN THE LAVA!



Controls

Press Spacebar jump!
Press Left or A to move left!
Press Right or D to move right!
Press Up or W to slow fall!
